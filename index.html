Neural Traces 2024 is a three-day onsite workshop on advanced data analysis methods in magneto- and electroencephalography (M/EEG) and their clinical applications in neurological and psychiatric disorders. The workshop will include invited talks as well as contributed oral and poster presentations. Registration is currently open.

We look forward to seeing you in Berlin!

Hosts

Neural Traces 2024 is jointly organized by the Brain and Data Science Group at Charité-Universitätsmedizin Berlin, the Machine Learning and Inverse Modeling Group at Physikalisch-Technische Bundesanstalt, Berlin, and the Uncertainty, Inverse Modeling and Machine Learning (UNIML) Group at Technische Universität Berlin.
